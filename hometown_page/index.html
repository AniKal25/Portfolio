Lagos

Lagos is a vibrant city and expats first arriving will most likely have their senses overwhelmed by the chaos, noise and traffic. The lifestyle in Lagos is fast paced and, as one of the fastest growing cities in Africa, hustle and bustle abound. There are both pros and cons to moving here.
Generally, the inhabitants of Lagos are friendly and welcoming and will help you if you ask for directions.
